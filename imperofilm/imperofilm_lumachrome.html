Imperofilm Lumachrome

Imperofilm's first recipe

Anytime. Anywhere.

Lumachrome has your back. The unique emulsion is tailored to provide satisfying color day or night, rain or shine, regardless of how your subject is lit. Cool off in the sun with enhanced blues and subtle whites, or get cosy indoors and warm up in the rich tungsten tones.

    The Recipe

  • Classic Chrome
  • Grain: Off
  • Color Chrome: Weak
  • Color Chrome Blue: Off
  • White Balance: Fluorescent Light 3

    • Blue: -5
  • Dynamic Range: 400%
  • Tone Curve:
    • Highlight +2
    • Shadow -1
  • Color: 0
  • Sharpness: -2
  • High ISO Noise Reduction: -2
  • Clarity: 0
